Game Testing Report – Open World Gameplay Analysis

Testing Type: Functional Testing, Gameplay Testing, Performance Testing


Overview

This report analyzes gameplay systems, user interface behavior, and performance across two major open-world games. The objective is to identify bugs, inconsistencies, and issues that may impact gameplay quality and player immersion.

Testing was conducted across multiple scenarios including free roam exploration, NPC interaction, mission gameplay, and environmental interaction.

Game Analysis: Grand Theft Auto V

Test Scenarios
Free roam exploration in urban environments
Vehicle driving, collisions, and physics behavior
NPC interaction in crowded areas
Mission-based gameplay


Bug Report 1: NPC Pathfinding Issue

Area: Open World

Steps to Reproduce:

Navigate to a crowded street
Stand near obstacles such as benches or poles
Observe NPC movement

Expected Result:
NPCs should navigate smoothly around obstacles

Actual Result:
NPC gets stuck or repeatedly walks into objects

Severity: Medium

Impact: Breaks realism and immersion




Bug Report 2: Vehicle Physics Inconsistency

Area: Driving Mechanics

Steps to Reproduce:

Drive at high speed
Collide with another vehicle or object

Expected Result:
Realistic collision physics

Actual Result:
Vehicle flips unnaturally or behaves inconsistently

Severity: Medium

Impact: Affects gameplay realism


Performance Observations
Frame drops in high-traffic areas
Minor lag during high-speed driving
Occasional rendering delays





Game Analysis: Red Dead Redemption 2

Test Scenarios
Open-world exploration (towns & wilderness)
NPC interaction and AI behavior
Horse riding and environmental interaction
Mission-based gameplay
Bug Report 1: Animation Clipping

Area: Character Interaction

Steps to Reproduce:

Interact with objects (doors, items)
Observe character animations

Expected Result:
Smooth and realistic animations

Actual Result:
Character hands or objects clip through surfaces

Severity: Low

Impact: Reduces visual quality and immersion




Bug Report 2: NPC Behavior Delay

Area: AI Interaction

Steps to Reproduce:

Interact with NPCs in crowded towns
Observe response timing

Expected Result:
Immediate and natural NPC reactions

Actual Result:
Delayed or unnatural responses

Severity: Low to Medium

Impact: Affects realism


Performance Observations
Frame drops in dense town areas
Slight stuttering during camera movement
Minor delays in asset loading



Testing Approach

Observed gameplay under different conditions
Tested edge cases and player interactions
Focused on realism, consistency, and performance


Conclusion

Both games provide highly immersive experiences with advanced gameplay systems. However, minor issues such as NPC behavior inconsistencies, physics glitches, and animation problems can impact overall quality. Structured testing and bug reporting help improve stability and enhance player experience.
